Skill Level:  intermediate

SIZE

To fit average size woman’s foot. For larger size foot, use larger size knitting needles or crochet hook. The length of the leg can be made longer by knitting or crocheting the leg section an inch or two longer. In the crocheted sock, the length of the foot can also be increased, making sure the top and bottom of the foot are the same length.

MATERIALS
• LION BRAND Wool-Ease
2 balls in color of your choice.
• Size 6 (4 mm) and 8 (5 mm) sets of double-pointed needles (dpns) for smaller size (Size 7 (4.5 mm) and 9 (5.5 mm) for larger size) OR SIZE TO OBTAIN GAUGE.

GAUGE

For smaller size: 18 sts + 26 rows = 4” (10 cm) with smaller needles and double strand. 
For larger size: 16 sts + 24 rows = 4” (10 cm) with larger needles and double strand.
BE SURE TO CHECK YOUR GAUGE.

NOTE
Work with two strands of yarn held together throughout.

STITCH EXPLANATION
skp Slip 1 st, k 1 st, pass slip st over

SOCKS (Make 2)

Ribbing
With smaller dpns, cast on 32 sts (divided as foll: 10 sts on Needle #1, 12 sts on Needle #2, 10 sts on Needle #3). Join, work around in k1, p1 rib for 12 rnds.

Leg
Change to larger dpns. Work in St st (k every rnd) until piece measures 6”.

Heel
Next rnd K8, put rem sts on Needle #2, turn.
Next row Slip 1, p7 plus 8 more sts from Needle #3. Divide instep sts evenly on 2 dpns to be worked later. Work heel flap in St st for about 2 1/2”, always slipping first st of each row, ending with a RS row. Note You should have 7 loops along the sides (1 loop for every 2 rows).

Turn heel

Next row Slip 1, p8, p2tog, p1, turn.
Next row Slip 1, k3, skp, k1, turn.
Next row Slip 1, p4, p2tog, p1, turn.
Next row Slip 1, k5, skp, k1, turn.
Next row Slip 1, p6, p2tog, p1, turn.
Next row Slip 1, k7, skp, k1, DO NOT TURN-10 sts rem.
Next rnd Pick up and k7 sts along side of heel plus 1 st at the intersection of the needles. K across instep sts onto one needle. Pick up and k 1 st at intersection at other side plus 7 sts along edge, plus k 5 sts from heel onto same needle. Note Beg of rnd should be at center of heel. Place the st on each side that picked up at intersection on instep needle.
Next row On Needle #1, k sts; on Needle #2, skp at beg of needle and k2tog at end of needle to dec those 2 extra sts (this prevents a hole that may form at this place).

Gussets
Rnd 1 K to within 3 sts of Needle #1, k2tog, k1; on Needle #2, k across, on Needle #3, k1, skp, k to end.
Rnd 2 K even.
Rep last 2 rnds until 32 sts rem. Work even in St st to desired length, leaving about 1½”-2” for toe shaping. (Note For Size 7 1/2 shoe, knit to 7 3/4” from back of heel before starting toe shaping.)

Toe
Rnd 1 K to within 3 sts of Needle #1, k2tog, k1, on Needle #2, k1, skp, k to within 3 sts of end, k2tog, k1; on Needle #3, k1, k2tog, k to end.
Rnd 2 K even.
Rep last 2 rnds until 16 sts rem. K first 4 sts of last rnd with Needle #3 and weave these 8 sts to 8 sts on instep needle.

ABBREVIATIONS:
beg = begin(ning)
foll = follow(s)(ing)
k = knit
p = purl
rem = remain(s)(ing)		 rnd(s) = round(s)
RS = right side
st(s) = stitch(es)
St st = stockinette stitch
tog = together
